History and music

  • Pixies is an American alternative rock band founded in Boston in 1986. We are known for our distinctive sound, which combines elements of punk rock, indie rock and psychedelia. Our music is an experiment that explores human nature and the power of music.

  • We met at a party at Boston University and immediately realized that we shared a common love of music. We started playing together and quickly gained popularity in the local music scene.

  • In 1988, we released our debut album, Surfer Rosa. The album was well received by critics and helped us to attract a wider audience.

  • In 1989, we released our second album, Doolittle. The album has become an alternative rock classic and features such iconic songs as "Debaser," "Where Is My Mind?" and "Monkey Gone to Heaven."

  • In 1993, we broke up due to creative differences, personal conflicts, and drug problems.

  • In 2004, we reunited and continue to tour all over the world.
